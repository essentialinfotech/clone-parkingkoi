What does Parking Kothay Family believe in?


01. The Mission

We fight for the mission with our soldiers; the Parking Kothay family of customers & employees; & dream of a Bangladesh where parking is easy, safe & cheap.

02. Family First

Parking Kothay is more than a company. It is a growing family of 2 million people across the country. If you’re a part of this family then remember just as you love your vehicle like your own child, we excel at keeping that child safe from harms way with the most innovative parking & automotive solutions.

03. Empowering Entrepreneurship

Other than securing safe parking spots & providing automotive solutions, we are creating entrepreneurs in every home. You can earn from USD 2000-3500 i.e. 16000-24000 TK every month just renting out your parking space.

04. Change Maker

We believe we are the change maker Bangladesh needs to tackle the ever-growing traffic & parking problem. This company’s ongoing effort to solve these two major issues will impact both national & global economy. Our initiative will soon put Bangladesh’s Start Up success story on the global map.
